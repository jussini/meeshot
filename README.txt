A joke (or gag) is a phrase or a paragraph with a humorous twist. It can be 
in many different forms, such as a question or short story. To achieve 
this end, jokes may employ irony, sarcasm, word play and other devices. 
Jokes may have a punchline that will end the sentence to make it humorous.
